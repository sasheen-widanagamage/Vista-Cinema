FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

  • How do I book movie tickets?

    You can book movie tickets online through our website or mobile app. Simply log in, choose your movie, select your showtime, and complete the booking.

  • Can I book tickets in advance?

    Yes, you can pre-book your tickets in advance to ensure you have a seat for your preferred movie and showtime.

  • How much does it cost to watch a movie?

    The ticket price varies depending on factors such as movie type (standard or 3D/IMAX), day of the week, and time of the show. Prices are shown during the booking process.

  • Is there a VIP seating option?

    Yes, we offer VIP seating with extra comfort, better views, and premium service. You can select VIP seats during the booking process.

  • Can I change or cancel my booking?

    Yes, you can modify or cancel your booking before the movie starts. Any changes or cancellations will depend on the cinema's cancellation policy.

  • What types of movies do you show?

    We show a variety of movies including the latest blockbusters, independent films, and special screenings like 3D and IMAX.

  • What is the age rating for movies?

    Movies have various age ratings like G, PG, PG-13, R, and NC-17. Age ratings are displayed when you select a movie during booking.

  • Are there discounts for group bookings?

    Yes, we offer group discounts for large bookings. You can inquire about group rates while booking or contact customer support for more details.

  • What food and beverages are available?

    Our cinema offers a wide selection of snacks, beverages, and gourmet food. You can buy popcorn, nachos, soft drinks, and more at the concession stand.

  • Do you offer 3D or IMAX screenings?

    Yes, we offer 3D and IMAX screenings for select movies. Please check the movie listing to see if a film is available in these formats.

  • Is parking available at Vista Cinema?

    Yes, there is ample parking available for moviegoers. We also offer a parking service for your convenience.

  • How can I contact customer support?

    Customer support is available 24/7. You can contact us via phone, email, or the contact form on our website for assistance.
